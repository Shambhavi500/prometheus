## 1. Description of Variation
Change UPS battery technology from VRLA (base design, Spec Rev B) to **Lithium-ion (LFP)** for
BAT-01…BAT-12. Driver: Helios standardisation on Li-ion for extended life (15 yr vs 10 yr),
reduced footprint and improved TCO, plus higher cycling tolerance.
