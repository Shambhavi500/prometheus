## 2. Business Case & Objectives

Helios requires 48 MW of concurrently-maintainable compute capacity to serve committed cloud
tenants in the South India availability zone. Atlas is engaged on a turnkey EPC basis to design,
procure, construct, and commission the facility to Uptime Tier III certification (Design +
Constructed Facility), meeting the target Ready-for-Service (RFS) dates without compromising the
concurrent maintainability requirement.

**Primary objectives**
1. Achieve Tier III Certification of Design Documents (TCDD) and Constructed Facility (TCCF).
2. Deliver Phase 1 (Data Hall 1, 12 MW) RFS by 30-Sep-2025.
3. Maintain design PUE ≤ 1.35 and comply with CEA / IE Rules and NBC (National Building Code of India 2016) fire/life-safety.
4. Zero Lost-Time Injuries (LTI) — see HSE Plan.
5. Full L1–L5 commissioning with Integrated Systems Test (IST) witnessed by Northbridge CxA.

## 3. Scope Summary (In / Out)

**In scope:** MV/LV electrical distribution, standby diesel generation, static UPS + battery
energy storage, mechanical cooling (chilled water), fire detection & suppression, BMS/EPMS/DCIM
controls, structured cabling containment, and full commissioning to RFS.

**Out of scope:** IT/network active equipment, server racks and white-space fit-out beyond
busway drops, tenant-specific rack PDUs, land acquisition, grid substation (utility scope).
